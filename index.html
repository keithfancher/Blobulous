Blobulous

Vital things:

1) You are Blob:

2) These fuckers hate you:

3) You will kill them.

Less vital things:

1) This is your targeting reticule:
Click and drag it to murder your foes.

2) More targets means more points. (Points are good.)

3) This is a powerup:
Powerups mean more simultaneous targets. More targets means more points. (Points are good.)

4) Gather enough powerups and you enter APESHIT MODE. All your points are doubled. (Points are good.)
